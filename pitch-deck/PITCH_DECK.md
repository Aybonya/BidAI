# BidAI Pitch Deck (15 Slides)

## Slide 1 - Title
BidAI: AI-Powered Field Intelligence Platform

## Slide 2 - Problem
Farmers and agri-teams lose yield and margin due to delayed detection of stress, fragmented data, and slow decision cycles.

## Slide 3 - Market Pain
- No single view for satellite indices + weather + recommendations
- Manual interpretation of NDVI/NDMI/NDRE is time-consuming
- Reports are hard to operationalize for field teams

## Slide 4 - Solution
BidAI combines satellite overlays, field-level analytics, and AI-generated agronomy reports in one workflow.

## Slide 5 - Product Demo (MVP)
- Field map and polygon management
- Index viewer (NDVI/NDMI/NDRE etc.)
- AI report generation (field report + season comparison)
- Embedded AI assistant (Mansur chibi)

## Slide 6 - Core Value Proposition
- Faster stress detection
- More actionable recommendations
- Better yield/risk visibility
- Decision support in local language (Kazakh)

## Slide 7 - Technology
- Frontend: React + Vite + Leaflet
- Backend: Node.js + Express
- AI: OpenAI Responses API with fallback logic
- Remote sensing: Sentinel/imagery integrations

## Slide 8 - AI Moat
- Agronomy-specific prompts and schema outputs
- Field-context-aware assistant workflow
- Iterative dataset and recommendation feedback loop

## Slide 9 - Business Model
- Subscription by farm area (hectares)
- Tiered plans by features and API volume
- Enterprise add-ons (team management, exports, integrations)

## Slide 10 - Unit Economics (Target)
- Predictable per-field AI/reporting cost
- Margin improvement via usage controls and caching
- Upsell from monitoring to advanced analytics

## Slide 11 - Go-To-Market
- Pilot farms and agri-consultants
- Regional distributor partnerships
- Demo-first B2B sales with measurable ROI cases

## Slide 12 - Competitive Positioning
BidAI focuses on local-language UX, practical recommendations, and simplified adoption for everyday field operations.

## Slide 13 - Roadmap
- Short term: report quality, reliability, user roles
- Mid term: operation tracking, alert automation, mobile optimization
- Long term: yield optimization and prescriptive agronomy modules

## Slide 14 - Traction / KPI Framework
- Active fields and weekly active users
- Report generation frequency
- Recommendation adoption rate
- Estimated prevented losses

## Slide 15 - Ask
Seeking pilot partners, strategic distribution, and investment to scale product, data coverage, and enterprise integrations.
